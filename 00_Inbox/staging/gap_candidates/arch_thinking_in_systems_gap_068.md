## Candidates bị bỏ sót

- [Concept] Reinforcing Feedback Loop: A system dynamic where a change triggers a response that amplifies the original change, potentially leading to instability or escalation.
- [Concept] Symptom-Relieving Policy: A policy that addresses the surface manifestations of a problem without tackling the root cause.
- [Concept] Diversification: A strategy to mitigate competitive disadvantage by shifting resources or activities to new areas.
- [Concept] Unilateral Disarm: A tactic to halt an escalating conflict by voluntarily withdrawing from a competitive action.
- [Concept] Drift to Low Performance: A system tendency to decline due to performance standards influenced by past negative results.
