## Candidates bị bỏ sót

- [Concept] Limiting Factor: A factor that restricts the rate of growth or output of a system, regardless of the abundance of other factors.
- [Entity] Justus von Liebig: A scientist who developed the 'law of the minimum' regarding plant growth.
- [Method] Forrester's Corporate-Growth Model: A systems model used at MIT that illustrates how growth creates shifting limits.
- [Relationship] Dynamic System: A system where growth and its environment coevolve, constantly altering the relative scarcity of factors.
- [Concept] Artificial Fertilizer: A manufactured substance used to supplement soil nutrients.
