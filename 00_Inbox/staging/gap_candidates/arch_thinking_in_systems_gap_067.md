## Candidates bị bỏ sót

- [Concept] Dynamic Equilibrium: <A state where inflow and outflow are equal, maintaining a constant stock level.>
- [Concept] Feedback Loop: <A closed chain of cause and effect influencing a system’s behavior.>
- [Concept] Reinforcing Feedback Loop: <A loop that amplifies change, leading to growth or collapse.>
- [Concept] Balancing Feedback Loop: <A loop that stabilizes a system, seeking a target value.>
- [Concept] Delays: <Time lags in feedback loops that can cause oscillations.>
- [Concept] Nonrenewable Resources: <Resources that are depleted faster than they are replenished.>
- [Concept] Renewable Resources: <Resources that are replenished naturally over time.>
- [Concept] Exponential Growth: <A rate of increase that accelerates over time.>
- [Concept] Limits to Growth: <The inevitable constraint on growth within a finite environment.>
- [Concept] Scenario Testing Models: <Tools for exploring potential future system behaviors based on defined scenarios.>
