## Candidates bị bỏ sót

- [Concept] Feedback Loop: <A cyclical process where a change in a system’s output affects its input, maintaining stability or driving change.>
- [Entity] Stock: <A level or quantity of something within a system that is subject to change.>
- [Entity] Flow: <The rate at which something moves into or out of a system, influencing the stock level.>
- [Mental Model] Control Mechanism: <A system’s inherent process for regulating stocks and flows to maintain desired states.>
- [Relationship] Information Link: <A connection that transmits data used to adjust flows and maintain stock levels.>
