## Candidates bị bỏ sót

- [Concept] Goal Definition: Clearly defining goals is crucial for system design to avoid unintended consequences.
- [Entity] GNP: A measure of national economic success, heavily criticized for its limitations in reflecting human welfare.
- [Method] System Design: Designing systems around poorly defined goals leads to undesirable outcomes.
- [Principle] Effort vs. Result: Confusing effort with desired results is a common system design mistake.
- [Mental Model] Feedback Loops: Balancing feedback loops are dependent on a well-defined goal.
- [Relationship] GNP & Welfare: GNP does not measure human welfare, highlighting a fundamental disconnect.
