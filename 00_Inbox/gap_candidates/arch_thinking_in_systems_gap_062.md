## Candidates bị bỏ sót

- [Concept] Systems Theory: <A framework for understanding complex interactions between elements within a system, emphasizing feedback loops and emergent behavior.>
- [Entity] Russell Ackoff: <A management theorist who popularized the concept of "messes" and systems thinking.>
- [Method] Systems Thinking: <A problem-solving approach that focuses on understanding the interconnectedness of elements within a system rather than isolated parts.>
- [Relationship] Structure-Behavior: <The core principle of systems theory, stating that a system's structure determines its behavior, and vice versa.>
- [Concept] Feedback Loops: <Processes where the output of a system influences its input, creating self-regulating or amplifying effects.>
- [Entity] Slinky: <A physical object used as an analogy to illustrate the concept of systems behavior and the role of external forces.>
