## Candidates bị bỏ sót

- [Entity] Hora: A watchmaker who implemented a hierarchical design for efficient watch production.
- [Entity] Tempus: A watchmaker who did not implement a hierarchical design, leading to inefficiencies.
- [Relationship] Subassembly: Hora’s watches were constructed with stable subassemblies of ten elements each.
- [Relationship] Information Links: Hierarchical systems reduce the amount of information each part of the system needs to track.
- [Relationship] Feedback Delays: Hierarchical systems minimize feedback delays through carefully designed differential information links.
- [Relationship] Reductionist Dissection: Taking apart systems at different hierarchical levels can be useful for study, but one must consider the overall hierarchical relationships.
