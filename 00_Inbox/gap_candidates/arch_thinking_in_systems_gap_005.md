## Candidates bị bỏ sót

- [Concept] Feedback Loop: A closed chain of causal connections where a stock level is adjusted based on its deviation from a desired value.
- [Entity] Stock: A level of a system that is subject to feedback control.
- [Mental Model] Homeostasis: The tendency of a system to maintain stability by counteracting changes.
- [Relationship] Balancing Feedback Loop: A type of feedback loop that actively seeks to maintain a stock at a specific value or range.
