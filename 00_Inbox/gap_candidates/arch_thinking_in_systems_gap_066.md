## Candidates bị bỏ sót

- [Concept] Archetype: A recurring pattern of behavior within a system.
- [Concept] Balancing Feedback Loop: A loop that actively reduces change within a system.
- [Concept] Bounded Rationality: A decision-making process constrained by system context.
- [Concept] Dynamic Equilibrium: A state of steady-state balance in a system.
- [Concept] Feedback Loop: A mechanism for adjusting system behavior based on its state.
- [Concept] Linear Relationship: A direct, proportional relationship between variables.
- [Concept] Nonlinear Relationship: A relationship where cause and effect are not proportional.
- [Concept] Reinforcing Feedback Loop: A loop that amplifies change within a system.
