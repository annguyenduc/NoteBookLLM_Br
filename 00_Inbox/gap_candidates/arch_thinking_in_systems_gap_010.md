## Candidates bị bỏ sót

- [Concept] Reinforcing Loop: A loop that amplifies a system’s behavior, driving growth or change.
- [Entity] Economy: A complex system with interconnected industries and feedback loops.
- [Method] Oscillatory Behavior: The tendency of systems to fluctuate between states due to feedback loops.
- [Relationship] Balancing Loop: A loop that counteracts reinforcing loops, ultimately constraining growth.
- [Concept] Limits-to-Growth Archetype: A common system structure where growth is eventually constrained by environmental factors.
- [Relationship] Feedback Loops: Interconnected loops that influence a system’s behavior, either reinforcing or balancing.
- [Concept] Renewable Stock: A resource that can be replenished by the environment.
- [Concept] Nonrenewable Stock: A resource that is finite and cannot be replenished.
