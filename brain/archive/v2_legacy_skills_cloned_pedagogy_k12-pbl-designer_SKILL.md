# k12-pbl-designer — PBL Training Designer (PRO ToT — Integrated)

> **Goal:** Design teacher training programs on Project-Based Learning (PBL) methodology. Buck Institute (PBLWorks) Gold Standard criteria are integrated into facilitation scenarios — not displayed as header labels.

## When to Activate

- Designing a training program to teach teachers how to facilitate PBL projects
- Creating a model cross-curricular project for teacher practice
- Building a PBL scenario kit for a subject department (Science, CS, Math)
- Training teachers to shift from "lecturing" to "facilitating project learning"

## Instructions

### Dual-Mode Logic (Mandatory)

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **Check Intent:** If the target is a **student K-12 project**, the agent must STOP and ask: "I detect you want to design a K-12 student project. Do you want Gold Standard Student mode or Teacher PBL Facilitation Training mode?"

### Integrated PBL Design Principles

- **No Labeling:** Do NOT write "Gold Standard Pillar 1." Design open-ended facilitation questions that let teachers self-discover the key pillars.
- **Evidence of Impact:** Focus on the shift from "doing it for students" to "guiding students to do it." Design activities that empower students (Student Voice & Choice).
- **Design Thinking Integration:** Weave design thinking into the problem-solving process of the project.

### Teacher Training Modules (Implicit)

| Module | Focus |
|--------|-------|
| **The Drive** | Create "Transformation Questions" that drive inquiry. |
| **Scaffolding Mastery** | Design the student support pathway (Guided → Independent). |
| **Evidence Assessment** | Score based on actual progress, not just the final product. |
| **Public Impact** | Plan exhibition events that create real value for the community. |

## Quality Gate (Red Flags)

- ❌ Over-teaching PBL theory (> 15 minutes of lecture at the start). Teachers should "do PBL inside the PBL course."
- ❌ Missing time and resource management templates for teachers to use in their own classrooms.
- ⚠️ Post-check: Always run `/output-contract` to audit for pedagogical standards.

## Example Triggers

- "Create a PBL facilitation scenario for a Science department."
