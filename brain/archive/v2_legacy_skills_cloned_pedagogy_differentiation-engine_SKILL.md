# differentiation-engine — Teacher Differentiator (PRO — Integrated)

> **Goal:** Design scaffolded learning pathways appropriate for differentiating adult learner profiles. UDL principles are integrated into the activity sequences — not displayed as labels.

## When to Activate

- Designing training for teachers with varied technology backgrounds
- Creating scaffolded tools for novice teachers entering STEAM
- Developing extension challenges for experienced STEAM educators
- Building differentiated pathways into a CPD (Continuous Professional Development) program

## Instructions

### Dual-Mode Logic (Mandatory)

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **Check Intent:** If the target is **differentiated student lessons**, the agent must STOP and ask: "I detect you want to design differentiation for students. Do you want Student mode (3-Tier) or Trainer differentiation mode?"

### Integrated Scaffolding Paths

| Path | Target | Focus |
|------|--------|-------|
| **Guided Mastery (Newcomers)** | Teachers with no prior STEAM tech background. | Provide templates, sample lesson plans, and scripted scenarios for confident first delivery. |
| **Strategic Synergy (Integrators)** | Teachers integrating tech into existing subjects. | Focus on resolving pedagogical friction when blending technology into traditional subjects. |
| **Pedagogical Design (Innovators)** | Experienced STEAM educators. | Challenge teachers to fully redesign their teaching framework for breakthrough educational impact. |

### Integrated Design Principles

- **No Labeling:** Do NOT write phrases like "Tier 1: Apprentice." Instead write "Intensive Support Pathway."
- **Evidence of Support:** Differentiate through **Seed Data** and **Open-ended practice tasks** of varying complexity.
- **Universal Design for Learning (UDL):** Provide multiple access channels (Video, Text, Interactive) so teachers can choose how they learn.

## Quality Gate (Red Flags)

- ❌ Differentiation materials too abstract — do not help teachers overcome specific technical barriers.
- ❌ Missing learner agency options for teachers to demonstrate competency in varied ways.
- ⚠️ Post-check: Always run `/output-contract` to audit for inclusion errors.

## Example Triggers

- "Design training scenarios for teachers with varying tech literacy levels."
- "Create a scaffold tool for first-time Robotics teachers."
- "Build an extension challenge for veteran STEAM master trainers."
