# steam-content-factory — ToT Slide & Handout Factory (PRO — Integrated)

> **Goal:** Design multi-media training materials for ToT programs (Slides, Manuals, Handouts). Multimedia learning and andragogy standards are integrated into conciseness and presentation style — not labeled.

## When to Activate

- Creating slide decks for teacher training workshops
- Producing trainer manuals with step-by-step facilitation scripts
- Building participant handouts that are practice tools, not theory summaries
- Batch-creating training content packages for multiple modules

## Instructions

### Dual-Mode Logic (Mandatory)

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **Check Intent:** If the target is **student worksheets or lab sheets**, the agent must STOP and ask: "I detect you want to create learning materials for students. Do you want Student mode (Worksheet/Lab) or Trainer mode (Slide & Manual)?"

### Integrated Content Modules

- **Minimalist Slide Layout:** Maximum 5 bullet points per slide. Never write framework names (e.g., "Multimedia Rule X"). Let the conciseness itself demonstrate quality.
- **Deep Speaker Scripting:** Speaker notes must contain pedagogical transition cues — not just framework label notes.
- **Actionable Handouts:** Do not summarize theory. Create worksheets where teachers fill in their own design outputs.
- **Narrative Excellence:** Build continuous storytelling across training slides — each slide connects to the next.

### Integrated Design Principles

- **No Labeling:** Do NOT write phrases like "Principle 1: Coherence." Delete all non-functional content from slides.
- **Facilitation Evidence:** Speaker Notes must always contain an open-ended facilitation question that prompts learner response.

## Quality Gate (Red Flags)

- ❌ Slide is too text-heavy or contains theoretical frameworks that don't serve the session's practical objective.
- ❌ Disconnection between Speaker Notes and the content displayed on the slide.
- ⚠️ Post-check: Always run `/output-contract` to audit for professional standards.

## Example Triggers

- "Create a teacher training slide deck on cross-curricular Robotics."
- "Design a Trainer Manual for an IoT workshop (ToT format)."
- "Participant handout for a STEM Inquiry-based learning course."
- "Batch-create training content for 5 AI education modules."
