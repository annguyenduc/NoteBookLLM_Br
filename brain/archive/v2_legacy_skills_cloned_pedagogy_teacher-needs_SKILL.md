# teacher-needs — Teacher Needs Analyst (PRO Andragogy)

> **Goal:** Analyze the persona and practical needs of teacher-learners (Trainers). Ensure training content "hits" the real barriers and motivations of adult professionals.

## When to Activate

- Designing a new training program and need to understand the target teacher audience
- Differentiating content for teachers at different proficiency levels
- Identifying barriers (technology fear, time pressure, institutional resistance) before designing solutions
- Aligning training to the UNESCO AI Competency Framework for Teachers

## Instructions

### UNESCO Competency Level Logic (Mandatory)

> [!IMPORTANT]
> Classify each teacher cohort into one of 3 UNESCO 2024 development levels before designing:
> 1. **Acquire:** Need basic concepts, ready-to-use tools, step-by-step checklists.
> 2. **Deepen:** Need pedagogical integration methods, impact assessment techniques, classroom scenario problem-solving.
> 3. **Create:** Need new design frameworks, deep case studies, organizational change leadership skills.

### 5 Analysis Dimensions (UNESCO Aspects)

| Dimension | What to Uncover |
|-----------|----------------|
| **Humanistic Mindset** | Barriers around the belief that AI replaces teachers. |
| **AI/STEAM Ethics** | Concerns about data privacy and classroom fairness. |
| **Foundation & Application** | Proficiency with currently available tools. |
| **AI Pedagogy** | How to embed AI/STEAM into specific subjects (Math, Science, CS). |
| **Professional Development** | Career advancement motivations, lifelong learning drive. |

### Teacher Persona Canvas (v5.0)

1. **Professional Goals:** Title ambitions, student achievement targets.
2. **Pain Points:** Tech anxiety, time pressure, parental resistance.
3. **Current Toolkit:** Tools already mastered (PowerPoint, Zoom, Canva).
4. **UNESCO Baseline:** Which of the 15 competency blocks are they at?
5. **SAMR/TPACK Level:** Current tech integration level (Substitution or Redefinition?).

## Quality Gate (Red Flags)

- ❌ Proposing "Create-level" solutions to teachers who are still at "Acquire" level (causes overwhelm).
- ❌ Missing analogies when explaining technical concepts to non-technical teachers.
- ❌ Ignoring real-world constraints about device availability and internet connectivity at the school site.
- ⚠️ Post-check: Always cross-reference results with `k12-shared-references` (Section 4).

## Example Triggers

- "Analyze the persona for middle-aged teachers new to generative AI."
- "Build a learning pathway from Acquire to Deepen for Computer Science teachers."
- "Identify the ethical barriers teachers have about using surveillance cameras in classrooms."
