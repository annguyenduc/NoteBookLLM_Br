# module-architect — ToT Training Module Designer (PRO — Integrated)

> **Goal:** Design professional Trainer-of-Trainers (ToT) programs. SAMR and TPACK standards are integrated into the nature of activity design — not displayed as labels.

## When to Activate

- Designing a teacher training module for STEAM/AI/Robotics
- Creating slides, trainer manuals, and participant handouts for a workshop
- Building scenario-based learning for Micro-teaching sessions
- Structuring a training program around Andragogy principles

## Instructions

### Dual-Mode Logic (Mandatory)

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **Check Intent:** If the target is **K-12 student content**, the agent must STOP and ask: "I detect you want to create content for students. Do you want Student mode (5E) or Teacher Training module mode?"

### Integrated Design Principles

- **No Labeling:** Do NOT write "SAMR Level: M" or "TPACK: C-P-T" in slides or manuals. These are embedded in the audit, not the document.
- **Scenario Leadership:** Design activities that force teachers to change the fundamental nature of their method (Redefinition) through real-world scenarios.
- **Synergy First:** Focus on the intersection: "How does this tool solve that specific pedagogical challenge?"

### Training Design Ratio (Andragogy 30-60-10)

- **Theory (30%):** Inspire & provide a framework (taught implicitly through examples).
- **Practice (60%):** **Hands-on & Micro-teaching.** Teachers must physically design a transformation product.
- **Feedback (10%):** Analyze real case studies.

### Training Output Structure

| Deliverable | Key Requirement |
|------------|----------------|
| **Course Objectives** | Focus on "Facilitating" & "Orchestrating" — not "Knowing" or "Understanding." |
| **Slide Outline** | Minimal text. **Speaker Notes must include** facilitation questions and scenario handling scripts. |
| **Trainer Manual** | Step-by-step facilitation guide for the trainer running the session. |
| **Participant Handout** | A practice tool, not a theory summary. Teachers fill it in; they don't read it. |

## Quality Gate (Red Flags)

- ❌ Printing SAMR/TPACK labels directly in teaching slides.
- ❌ Practice ratio for adult learners < 60% of total session time.
- ❌ Missing constraints in exercises that would push creative problem-solving.
- ⚠️ Post-check: Always run `/output-contract` for implicit validation.
