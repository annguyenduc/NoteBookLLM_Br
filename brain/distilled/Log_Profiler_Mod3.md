**CREATE Framework:**

The CREATE framework is a detailed structure for crafting prompts to control AI output. It consists of six components:

1. **Character (C)**: Defines the role or persona the AI should assume.
2. **Request (R)**: Specifies the task or action the AI should perform.
3. **Examples (E)**: Provides examples or references to help the AI understand the context and requirements.
4. **Adjustment (A)**: Allows for fine-tuning or adjusting the AI's response to better fit the needs.
5. **Type (T)**: Indicates the type of response or output expected from the AI.
6. **Extra info (E)**: Includes any additional information or context that might be relevant to the task.

**Application in High School Education:**

The CREATE framework can be applied in high school education to help students control AI output and achieve their learning objectives. For instance, when working on a project, students can use the CREATE framework to craft prompts that guide the AI in providing relevant information, examples, and explanations.
